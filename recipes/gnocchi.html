Gnocchi

  • 3 Russet Potatos
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Flour

Steps

  1. Boil Potatos until fork tender
  2. Mash them up into mashed potatos
  3. Add Flour and Egg. Knead until a supple dough forms. Not too sticky, not too dry.
  4. Divide the dough into 4 pieces, roll those pieces into long snakes
  5. Cut those snakes into 1 inches pieces
  6. Put those pieces into a Boiling pot of water and cook them for 90 seconds
  7. Get a pan with a little bit of oil, and give them a crust
